Yuankai FAN

PostDoc, Computer Science

Fudan University

Hi there! I am presently serving as a postdoctoral researcher at Fudan University, collaborating with Prof. X. Sean Wang. I earned my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science from Fudan University, where I was also advised by Prof. X. Sean Wang, and Prof. Zhenying He.

My main research interest is Natural Language Interfaces to Databases. I am currently working on natural language to SQL translation using a new generate-and-rank approach, and I am also exploring how to explain natural language query results in natural language. Free feel to reach out if you’d like to chat! 👏
